Welcome To Eye Care

Eye Care is a centre committed to quality eye treatments at an affordable cost. It is equipped with all the ultramodern OPD machines and HI-Tech Operation Theatre. Dr Anju Gupta, Chief Consultant Eye Surgeon here is a dedicated, soft-spoken and skilled eye surgeon who has expertise in cataract surgery especially by stitchless small incision (SICS) technique and Micro Incision Phaco (MICS) technique. She is performing all types of Lasik Lasers with consistently good results.
